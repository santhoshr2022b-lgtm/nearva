About Nearva

Nearva is a local service discovery platform that helps people find trusted workers nearby instantly.

Our Goal

Our goal is simple: Connect customers directly with local workers without middlemen. We believe help should be easy to find, fast to connect, and simple to use.

What We Do

  • Discover nearby service providers
  • View availability in real-time
  • Contact workers directly

We are building Nearva with a strong focus on simplicity, transparency, and local trust.

We are continuously improving the platform to serve communities better.
